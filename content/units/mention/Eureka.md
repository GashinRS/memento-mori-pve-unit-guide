When Eureka is defeated, she grants the highest attack ally a 50% attack buff when this is an Azure ally. She has an
additional support effect where she can cleanse herself and the 2 highest attack allies when Eureka is debuffed. While
neither of these effects are particularly powerful (given that you are forced to run an Azure DPS, i.e. Florence), she
can be a good budget support option in the early game given that she does not require her UW or extra copies.

**Speed Tuning —** No speed tuning is required
