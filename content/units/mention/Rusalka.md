Rusalka grants 5 Multi-Barriers to herself and 1 to every ally when she is defeated. This is useful as a one time increase in 
survivability, but she gets outdone by Meria, who can provide Multi-Barriers every turn without requiring her to be defeated.

**Speed Tuning —** No speed tuning is required
